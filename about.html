About Us

Welcome to StaticNova — a small independent project created for students who want easier access to useful study resources and educational content.

StaticNova is designed as a community-driven platform where students can discover and share materials such as AI-generated reviews, exam leaks, formula sheets collected from public internet sources, and other academic resources that may help during studies and exam preparation.

The project is currently managed by a single team member: @paperstreetghost.

In addition to study resources, StaticNova also features a dedicated Music section where users can explore high-quality albums and full-track collections available through the Teams Music Vault channel. Users can also access external listening platforms such as Spotify and YouTube Music directly from the site.

We value community feedback and continuously aim to improve the platform. If you have ideas, suggestions, questions, or concerns regarding copyright or content ownership, please contact us at:
